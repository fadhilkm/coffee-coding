Espresso

Espresso is a type of coffee produced by extracting ground coffee beans by spraying hot water under high pressure. Espresso comes from Italian which means express or "fast" because it is made to be served immediately to customers.

Latte

A caffè latte is espresso or coffee mixed with milk and has a thin layer of foam on top. The ratio between milk and coffee in a caff latte is 2:1

Americano

Americano is an espresso coffee drink with the addition of hot water. Its name is taken from the way Americans drink espresso. That said, the mention of americano is a joke and a mockery of Americans who like their espresso made more diluted.
